Welcome to jsPaint

This JavaScript based paint application is the afford of collaborative work of some people who gathered to learn and practise programming (and chose JavaScript as their favorite language).

Caused by its nature as learning project, this application may be at any time work in progress. Although be aware: The source may not always be the best poetry, but all contributors are working hard to improve.
